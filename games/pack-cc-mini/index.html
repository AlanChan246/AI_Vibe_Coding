Five in a row

5×5 board. Take turns placing pieces. The first player to claim a full row, column, or diagonal wins.

Rules

  • White moves first, then Black, like chess colors.
  • A winning line is exactly five cells in a row — on this board, that is a full row, full column, or main diagonal.
  • If every square is filled with no winner, the game is a draw.
