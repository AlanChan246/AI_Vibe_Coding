Block Lab

Build voxel rooms, then play a light life-sim layer: household funds, a day clock, six needs meters, and furniture blocks that refill them when you stand nearby. Press Tab for Live/Build, B for the buy catalog, Friends and Career on the right dock.

Uses mouse look · works best with a mouse. After play, if the view does not turn with the mouse, click once on the 3D view; opening the site over http://localhost avoids some browser limits.
