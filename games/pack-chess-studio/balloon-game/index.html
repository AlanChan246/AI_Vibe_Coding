Balloon Zoo

Inflate, twist, and sculpt a cartoon balloon animal!

  1. Pick which balloon animal you want to sculpt.
  2. Hold Inflate until the meter is in the green zone, then release. Too little or too much pops the balloon!
  3. Click each pulsing twist in order — the balloon folds into your animal as you go.
  4. Finish all twists to reveal your animal. Play again to try another shape.
